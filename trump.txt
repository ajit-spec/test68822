Donald Trump served as the 45th President of the United States from 2017 to 2021.
He was a controversial figure known for his business background, reality TV career, and unconventional political style.
Before entering politics, Trump was a real estate developer and television personality, hosting "The Apprentice" from 2004 to 2015.
His presidency was marked by significant policy changes, including tax reforms, immigration restrictions, and his response to the COVID-19 pandemic.

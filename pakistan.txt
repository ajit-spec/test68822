Pakistan is a country in South Asia, known for its diverse landscapes from the Himalayas to the Arabian Sea.
The nation has a rich history dating back to ancient civilizations and is home to important archaeological sites.
Pakistan is also known for its diverse culture, including the vibrant music and dance traditions of Punjab, the rich cuisine of Karachi, and the ancient city of Mohenjo-Daro.
The country's national language is Urdu, though English is also widely used as an official language.

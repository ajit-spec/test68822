India is the seventh-largest country by land area and the most populous democracy in the world.
The country is known for its rich cultural heritage, diverse languages, and ancient civilization spanning thousands of years.
It is also recognized for its contributions to mathematics, including the invention of zero and the decimal system.
The Indian subcontinent is home to various geographical features, from the Himalayas in the north to tropical beaches in the south.
Indian cuisine is famous worldwide for its diverse flavors, spices, and regional variations.
